SECTION: Licensing Requirements to Operate Altec Equipment

CDL Requirements:
- Most Altec aerial trucks and digger derricks exceed 26,001 GVWR
- Class B CDL minimum for single-axle models (33,000-45,000 GVWR)
- Class A CDL for tandem-axle heavy models
- Air brake endorsement required on virtually all models
- Medical examiner certificate (DOT physical, 2-year renewal)

OSHA and Safety:
- ANSI A92.2 certification for aerial device operators (required for vehicle-mounted work platforms)
- OSHA 29 CFR 1910.269 for line-clearance work within approach distances of energized lines
- OSHA 29 CFR 1926.1400 series for construction use
- Annual aerial device inspection required (ANSI A92.2)
- Manufacturer training recommended (Altec offers training programs)

Business/Insurance:
- General liability insurance $1M-$5M (utilities typically require $2M+)
- Commercial auto for the vehicle ($5,000-$15,000/year for an aerial truck)
- Workers compensation
- Performance bonds for utility/government contracts
