About Equipment Finance Hub

Independent guides that explain how commercial equipment financing works — so you can compare options with lenders and advisors. We are not a bank and we do not lend money.

Last updated: March 25, 2026

What we publish

Equipment Finance Hub collects and explains financing concepts, typical price and payment ranges, brand and model context, licensing and business requirements, and industry terminology. Pages are built for operators, owners, and buyers researching commercial equipment — from machine tools and forklifts to heavy construction and specialized assets.

Where we reference regions, states, or basins (for example in energy or forestry content), it is to reflect how markets and regulations often differ — not to guarantee results in any one jurisdiction.

What we are not

  • We are not a lender, broker, or credit decision-maker.
  • We do not guarantee rates, approvals, or payment amounts.
  • Content is not legal, tax, or investment advice; consult qualified professionals for your situation.

Affiliate & partner links

Some pages include links to financing partners (for example, “Get financing” buttons). Those may be commercial relationships. They do not change our editorial goal: clear, accurate, reader-first explanations. Always compare multiple lenders before committing.

Geographic focus

Guides are written primarily for United States readers: U.S. terminology, currency, and common regulatory references. Requirements vary by state and industry — verify licenses and obligations with state agencies and qualified counsel.
